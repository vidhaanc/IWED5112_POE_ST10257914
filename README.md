Ubuntu Coffee Roasters is a small local coffee business located in Johannesburg that specialises in ethically sourced African coffee beans and handcrafted beverages. The business currently relies mainly on walk in customers and word of mouth marketing. Developing a website will allow the business to establish an online presence, promote its brand identity, and reach a wider audience beyond its physical location.
A website is an essential marketing tool because it enables businesses to communicate information, build brand awareness, and interact with potential customers online. According to Chaffey and Ellis-Chadwick (2019), a well designed website helps organisations improve their visibility, communicate their value proposition, and support customer engagement.
The Ubuntu Coffee Roasters website will act as a digital storefront where customers can learn about the company, explore available products, view the café environment, and contact the business. It will also provide a platform for future expansion such as online ordering or event bookings.
In the modern digital economy, consumers often research businesses online before visiting them physically. A website therefore increases credibility and allows the company to compete more effectively in the marketplace (Ryan, 2020).
