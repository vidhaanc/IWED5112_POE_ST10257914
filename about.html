About Our Business

Ubuntu Coffee Roasters is a Johannesburg-based café that specialises in ethically sourced African coffee beans. The business was founded with the goal of promoting African coffee culture and supporting local farmers.

Our mission is to provide high-quality coffee while creating a welcoming space where people can connect, work, and relax. We believe that coffee is more than just a drink – it is a cultural experience that brings people together.
